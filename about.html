About Us

We are passionate travelers who love to explore the world and share our experiences with others.

Our Story

What started as a simple travel diary has now become a full-fledged travel blog, where we document our adventures, provide tips, and share insights with fellow travelers. From the beaches of Bali to the mountains of Switzerland, we have covered a wide range of destinations, each with its own unique charm.


Our journey began in 2015, and since then, we have traveled to over 30 countries, exploring hidden gems and popular spots alike. We believe in responsible travel and always strive to respect local cultures and environments.
